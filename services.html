Basement Floor Epoxy Coating in Omaha

Basement floor epoxy coating in Omaha turns a raw, unfinished slab into a clean, usable surface for a home gym, workshop, rec room, or storage space. Omaha basements take a beating — older homes especially deal with moisture seeping through aging slabs, and bare concrete holds dust, absorbs stains, and looks unfinished no matter how clean it is. We test for moisture before every basement floor coating job in Omaha, because hydrostatic pressure (water pushing up from below) needs to be addressed before any coating goes down. If the slab passes, we apply a full epoxy system that seals the surface, resists moisture from above, and gives the floor a professional finish.

Decorative Flake Epoxy Flooring in Omaha

Decorative flake epoxy flooring in Omaha combines the durability of a full epoxy system with a custom look that covers minor surface imperfections and adds texture underfoot. Color chips are broadcast into the wet base coat, then locked under a clear topcoat — the result is a surface that looks like polished stone, hides dirt between cleanings, and is less slippery than solid-color epoxy when wet. Decorative flake epoxy flooring is the most popular choice for Omaha garages because it's as durable as standard epoxy but looks finished rather than industrial. Epoxy Floor Repair and Resurfacing in Omaha

Epoxy floor repair and resurfacing in Omaha addresses concrete that's cracked, pitted, or has a failing existing coating — without requiring a full slab replacement. We fill cracks and divots, grind down any failing coatings, and resurface the slab with a fresh epoxy system. If your previous epoxy or paint coating is peeling, bubbling, or delaminating — common in Omaha garages where a paint-based or water-based coating was applied without proper prep — resurfacing gets the floor back to new condition. Not every floor is a resurfacing candidate; if the underlying concrete has structural movement or major heaving, we'll tell you upfront.
